Donate to Invictus Woods

I.W.E.C. Inc. (Invictus Woods Endowment Corporation) is a non-profit, 501C3 organization. I.W.E.C. Inc.'s mission is to provide resources to help individuals further their recovery from chemical dependency in a safe, secure, sober environment. Honor a loved one and help support sober living in the Midwest by making a contribution that can be directed to one of the following:

  • Invictus Scholarships
  • Staff Development
  • Invictus Recreational Fund
  • Named Scholarship Funds
  • In memory of loved ones lost to addiction

All contributions are tax deductible and will receive letter of acknowledgement.
